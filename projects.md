		<div class="project-item">
			<div class="project-item-header">
				<p class="project-name"><i class="fa fa-code" aria-hidden="true"></i> tweetcost.com</p>
				<p class="project-launch">September, 2015</p>
			</div>
			<img class="project-image" src="/images/projects/tweetcost.png"></img>
		
			<div class="project-description">
				<p>A web app experiment to keep track of my personal finances by openly sharing it with the world. Tweetcost was a single-page-app that featured a large counter which would update every 2 minutes.</p>

				<p>The idea was that I would tweet out the prices of my expenses as I traveled and meteor would query Twitter's api every 2 minutes crawling through any new posts made by me since the last scan.</p>

				<p>Any new tweets with prices included and a designated hashtag would be returned as JSON and parsed by the app and stored in the backend. The front end would total the new cost and display it.</p>
				<p>The app is no longer live.</p>
			</div>
